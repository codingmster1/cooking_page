Ramen Cup:

These usually come with instructions on the package itself but here is a run down:

  1. Open Lid.
  2. Pour Powder from Packet into Cup.
  3. Fill the Cup Halfway With Water.
  4. Microwave then Stir.

If you happen not to like packaged food, scroll down and feed it to your cat. No, I will not give you steps on how to do that. Please adore the beautiful kitty.

Rice Ball:

These are almost always commonly seen in any series you watch. Only this time, with instructions and links to recipes.

Overview:

  • Prep: 5 Minutes
  • Cook: 30 Minutes
  • Total: 35 Minutes
  • Servings: 6 to 8
  • Yield: 8 Riceballs

Nutrition Facts:

  • Calories: 119
  • Fat: 1g
  • Carbs: 25g
  • Protein: 2g

Ingredients:

  • 1 to 2 sheets dried nori seaweed
  • 4 Cups of Steamed Japanese Rice
  • Kosher salt
  • 1 ounce black sesame seeds

Fillings:

  • 1 umeboshi
  • 1 fillet salmon
